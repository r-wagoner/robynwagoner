Something About Robyn Wagoner

There's something you should probably know.

From a young age, Robyn has demonstrated "rebellious tendencies". Perhaps this originates with her name - a reference to the Olde English, Robin Goodfellow, Hobgoblin, or Puck - a mischievous faery trickster.

Or perhaps the reason lies in her astrological sign, Libra, which is known for acting as the Devil's advocate, heckling those in authority, and serving as the class clown and general upstart.

A fan, artist, and muse of underground, experimental art, she was immediately drawn to protest music, punk rock, and hip-hop for their challenging of societal norms. At 18-years-old, Robyn was the first female roadie for Van Halen, pioneering women's technical positions in the music industry. A close friend once coined the moniker "The Instigator" to describe Robyn's character arc and motivation. People tend to love her or hate her. This information may discourage you from reaching out in friendship, especially if you're shy. Yet don't discount Robyn too quickly, for although she is known as a wily opponent, you rarely find a more loyal ally.

Robyn's path is a strange and difficult one, producing volumes of material for her stand-up comedy routines where she happily mocks notions of power normalcy.
